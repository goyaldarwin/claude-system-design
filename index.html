System Design — Principal Engineer Track

A self-contained, fully interlinked system design library: the classic Grokking lessons plus a principal-level extension covering distributed systems theory, data-intensive systems, modern architecture, reliability, security, advanced design problems, and a self-assessment guide.

72 lessons + self-assessment guide, with ByteByteGo diagrams embedded in relevant lessons · fully offline · portable (copy the folder anywhere, links keep working).

New here? Start at lesson 01, jump to the self-assessment guide & full index.
The core idea, in one line: every system is finite, mortal computers connected by a slow, unreliable network, under a growing workload — and every technique here is a way of paying one of the six bottlenecks that forces. Start with lesson 01 for the foundation the whole course builds on.
